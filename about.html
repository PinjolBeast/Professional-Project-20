Our Story

Born from a Love of Flowers

Bloom & Blush began with a simple dream: to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories through the beauty of flowers.

Founded in 2015 by floral designer Emma Rose, our studio started as a small passion project in her grandmother's garden. Today, we've grown into a beloved destination for those seeking authentic, handcrafted floral arrangements that speak from the heart.

Every bouquet we create is a testament to our belief that flowers are more than decorations—they're messengers of emotion, carriers of memory, and symbols of life's most precious moments.
